# DJI Mini 2 Teardown Notes

## Purpose

Document physical observations, part markings, connector locations, board layout, and repair notes for DJI Mini-class aircraft used in the Drone Libre preservation effort.

## Aircraft Scope

Initial focus:

- DJI Mini / Mini SE / Mini 2 family research
- Crashed or non-airworthy donor aircraft
- Repairable aircraft used for lawful diagnostics and preservation
- Comparison between OEM hardware and proposed open companion or replacement modules

## Documentation Goals

- Photograph and label each board, connector, antenna, sensor, and cable route.
- Record connector pin counts and cable orientation before disassembly.
- Identify replaceable assemblies such as motors, gimbal parts, antennas, GPS, ESC, flight controller, and power board.
- Track known-good donor parts separately from damaged or unknown parts.
- Capture measurements needed for adapter boards, enclosures, backpacks, and replacement flight-controller concepts.
